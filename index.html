Tower of Terror

This is a picture of me, taken in December of 2017, in front of my favorite ride at Disney World, titled Tower of Terror, located in Disney's Hollywood Studios. The ride takes place in Hollywood in 1939. A child actress, her nanny, a bellhop, and a prominent acting couple traveled in a doomed elevator. A lighting attack struck when these six people were in the elevator shaft, immediately killing these unlucky six, who became ghosts and haunt the hotel. The hotel closed down after this nightmare. When you enter the ride, you find yourself in the old hotel, in the same elevator shaft that they were in when they were killed. You are dropped up and down in the same elevator. Once, I rode this seven times in a row! I love this ride.
